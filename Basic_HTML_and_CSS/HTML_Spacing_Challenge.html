Raccoons

Raccoons are mammals that are found throughout North America, South America, and parts of Europe.
They have gray and black fur and black around their eyes.
Raccoons live up to 16 years old.

Opossums and Possums

Opossums are marsupials that are found throughout Eastern United States and Central America.
Possums are marsupials that are found through Australia and New Guinea.
Opossums have gray, white, and black fur with a white face and pink nose.
Possums have gray fur.
